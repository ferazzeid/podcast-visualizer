## How to Use New Features

### Save Your Settings as a Preset
1. Configure your visualization exactly how you want
2. Scroll to the **Presets** section
3. Click **"Save Current Settings"**
4. Name your preset and click Save

### Load a Preset
1. Go to the **Presets** section
2. Click any preset name to load it instantly
3. All settings (colors, style, sizes) will update

### Try the New Gentle Styles
1. In **Visualization Style** section
2. Look under **"Gentle & Relaxed"**
3. Try "Breathe" or "Flowing Wave" for calm podcasts
4. "Orbital" and "Ripples" for elegant, professional look

## Settings Location
Your settings are saved in browser localStorage:
- Key: `podcast-visualizer-settings`
- Presets Key: `podcast-visualizer-presets`

## OBS Integration (Unchanged)
Still works perfectly:
1. Add Browser Source: `http://localhost:5173?obs=true`
2. Size: 1920x1080
3. Enable transparent background in settings
4. Layer over your webcam
